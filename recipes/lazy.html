Lazy meal

Description

The perfect solution for those lazy days, especially if you live in a big city!

Ingredients

  • Variery of food apps to your liking
  • 1x large apettite
  • 1x credit card

Steps

  1. Launch your food delivery app. This can be done from your couch or even your bed
  2. Browse the catalogue to find out what looks appealing today. How about some mexican, or chinese? You can never go wrong with pizza or ramen.
  3. Pick a (few) items and remember to account for your hunger. I always end up ordering too much!
  4. Set your address and credit card details.
  5. Get over the shock of the fees, taxes, and tips applied to your order. Order it anyway, you are hungry and already spent way too much time picking something out.
  6. Follow the app tracking and the delivery driver to your address.
  7. Set the table in anticipation of your food.
  8. Open your door once your delivery arrives. Remember to put on some pants, if needed.
  9. Enjoy your lazy meal!
