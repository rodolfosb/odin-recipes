Caprese Salad

Description

Delicious, fresh, quick, and easy to make salad. Perfect for warm summer days.

Ingredients

  • 1x large beefsteak tomato
  • 1x Mozzarella ball
  • A handful of basil leaves
  • Reduced balsamic vinegar
  • Flaky sea salt
  • (Optional) Dry oregano

Steps

  1. Wash the tomato and basil leaves, and drain the liquid from the mozzarella package
  2. Slice the tomato and mozzarella evenly, approximately 0.5cm slices
  3. On your serving plate, arrange the tomato slices, mozzarella slices, and basil leaves and repeat until all of the tomato and mozzarella are used.
  4. Drizzle the balsamic vinegar over the dish
  5. If you are using the dry oregano, lightly sprinkle it over the dish
  6. Add flaky sea salt to taste
  7. Enjoy!
